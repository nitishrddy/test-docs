## Overview

Rally Engage is a highly configurable health engagement platform that helps clients drive healthy behaviors. With high level of personalization at both a client and member-level, members are rewarded for activities that truly benefit them. Members earn Rally Points in addition to these client defined rewards for participating, which can then be redeemed in the Rally Rewards marketplace for offers, donations, auctions, and sweepstakes. Promotions such as Spotlight Campaigns and Top Picks surface relevant content, while Benefits Cards & Resource Links highlight eligible programs and helpful links.
